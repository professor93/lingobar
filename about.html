LingoBar

v0.1.0

Instant translations from your menu bar.

LingoBar uses no API, sends no requests, and parses nothing — it opens the official Google Translate site in a webview, like a browser would, and hides everything outside the translator.

Google Translate is a trademark of Google LLC. LingoBar is an independent, unofficial client, not affiliated with or endorsed by Google.
